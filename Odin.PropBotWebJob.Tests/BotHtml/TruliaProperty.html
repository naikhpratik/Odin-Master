Description

This 3 bedroom ranch home with attached garage offers great space, meticulous maintenance and a stunning backyard. Single floor living with open concept, living, dining, family room and kitchen. Really fantastic living and entertaining space between the formal front room and large family room with vaulted ceiling and exposed beams. Kitchen features newer flooring and is open to both the family room and dining room. Updated fixtures in most rooms and ceiling fans in the family room, kitchen and two of the three bedrooms. Bath remodeled '11 with tile, vanity and fixture, toilet and mirror. Laundry room also with newer flooring, is directly off the attached garage and has plenty of space for extra storage or to convert to a mudroom. Newer carpet in the living and dining room, hall and 2nd bedroom, HWT 2015, electric panel 2016, front window and family room slider 2014. Vinyl siding 2013 with 10yr warranty. Backyard has a lovely deck and walkway, large shed, fully fenced and beautifully landscaped. Fall in love with this lovingly cared for home! Available early December.
